718 Cayman S

Porsche

The 718 models were built for those who feel a passion for sport. They are mid-engined sports cars that combine the sporting spirit of the legendary Porsche 718 with the sports car of the future – and bring them to the roads of the world. With one goal: to take the routine out of everyday life.

296 GTB

Ferrari

The 296 GTB, an evolution of Ferrari’s mid-rear-engined two-seater sports berlinetta concept, represents a revolution for the Maranello-based company as it introduces the new 120° V6 engine coupled with a plug-in (PHEV) electric motor capable of delivering up to 830 cv. The car thus defines the idea of driving fun to provide pure excitement not only when pursuing maximum performance but also in everyday driving.

Huracán

Lamborguini

The Huracán is equipped with a powerful V10 engine and the latest technologies to perform at its absolute best. Engineered to guarantee the best performance and control under any driving condition, it's easy to take to the limit, not to mention extremely fun, thanks to technology like the Lamborghini Doppia Frizione (LDF) dual clutch gearbox and the electronically-controlled four-wheel drive. Enjoy the best driving experience, ever.
